Page 2 · The Approach
Our Approach
The Way
We Work
I shoot every wedding we take on. There is no B team and no associate sent in my place.

When you book Tale of Two, you are booking me, which is why we hold a limited number of dates each season.

We work in a documentary style. Most of what matters at a wedding is never staged: your father adjusting his glasses just before the muhurtham, your friends failing to hold it together during the vidaai. We build the story of your day from those moments, not from a shot list.

Before the wedding, we spend time getting to know you both. This is not a formality. Knowing how the two of you are around each other tells me where to stand and when to wait. It also means you forget the camera faster on the day itself.

What you receive at the end is not a folder of images. It is an honest record of one of the few days you will want to return to for the rest of your life.

— Sumenth, Founder & Photographer

CANDID PHOTOS

A hand-picked set of candid photographs, edited one by one in our signature style. Weddings of this scale typically receive 600 to 900 final images.

Complete conventional coverage of every ritual and every guest, uploaded to your private online gallery.

CANDID TEASER

A 2 to 4 minute trailer of your wedding, cut to the moments that defined it.

Your full wedding story as a 10 to 20 minute documentary film, built around real sound from the day: the vows, the speeches, the conversations in between.
